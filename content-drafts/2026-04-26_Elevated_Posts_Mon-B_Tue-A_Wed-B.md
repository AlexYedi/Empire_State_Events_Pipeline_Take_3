## Cross-post elevation pattern (for any future variant)

The same five moves apply:

1. **State the conclusion in 1 line, then deliver evidence.** (Research)
2. **Name the singular five-second moment** — the instant of transformation, not a meandering setup. (Storytelling)
3. **Memify one repeatable phrase per post.** Test: can the reader repeat it at a dinner party tomorrow? (Storytelling)
4. **Convert vague "so what" into a specific operational consequence** the reader has to grapple with. (Research)
5. **Flag confidence on time-shift / market-shift claims** with `~`, "approximately," or explicit caveats. (Research)
