### Variant A — "Three vantage points on one role" (panel-design thesis)

> FDE is the most talked-about title in AI startups.
>
> Monday it actually gets defined — by the people doing it, not the people writing about it.
>
> Acacia Consulting brought three operators to 6 St Johns Ln with deliberately non-overlapping vantage points on one role:
>
> → Caitlin Leksana — Co-Founder, Fazeshift (YC, AR agents, seed)
> → Jordan DeLoach — FDE, Scaled Cognition (APT, agent-native foundation model, seed+)
> → Kamesh Vedula — AI/ML, Rippling (cross-functional agents at platform scale)

> • How FDE work differs at the model layer (shipping alongside APT) vs. the app layer
> • How Rippling sequences FDE-heavy onboarding into scaled AE motion past the first 10 customers
>
> Most FDE takes are analyst-layer. Monday is operator-layer. Bringing pen and paper.
>
> #ForwardDeployedEngineer #AINative #GTM

### Variant B — "The role diverges" (definition-drift thesis)

> The most in-demand role in AI startups is also the most loosely defined.
>
> "Forward Deployed Engineer" means five different things depending on the room you're in. Monday at 6 St Johns Ln, Acacia Consulting puts three of those rooms on one panel:
>
> → Seed founder running FDE-heavy GTM (Caitlin Leksana, Fazeshift)
> → Field FDE at a model-layer company (Jordan DeLoach, Scaled Cognition)
> → AI/ML leader at a platform running FDE-at-scale (Kamesh Vedula, Rippling)
>
> The divergence is the story. Role specs at seed are not role specs at platform. The FDE Caitlin would hire is not the FDE Jordan is, and neither is the FDE motion Kamesh is scaling. Most job descriptions flatten this into one bullet list — which is why most FDE hires go sideways in the first six months.
>
> What I'm listening for:
> • The tell that "the founder is the FDE" has run its course
> • Whether FDE skills transfer from app-layer to model-layer companies, or if the role fundamentally reboots
> • Where the FDE-to-AE handoff actually works vs. where it creates the customer-success vacuum you then have to fill
>
> FDE is the GTM primitive of this startup era. Worth getting the definition right.
>
> #ForwardDeployedEngineer #AINative #GTMBuilder

**Thesis:** The title is the most loosely defined important role in AI right now; the divergence across stages IS the conversation.
